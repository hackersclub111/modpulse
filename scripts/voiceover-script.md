# ModPulse Demo Video — Voiceover Script
# Duration: ~90 seconds
# Tone: Confident, fast-paced, winner energy

---

## SCENE 1: The Problem (10s)
**SCREEN**: Reddit homepage, zoom into r/modqueue with 500+ items
**VOICEOVER**:
"Every day, Reddit moderators face a flood of content. Manually checking each user's profile, history, and behavior takes ten minutes per user. Five hundred items in the mod queue? That's eighty-three hours of manual work. No team can keep up."

---

## SCENE 2: Enter ModPulse (8s)
**SCREEN**: Navigate to r/modpulse_intel_dev, show the installed app
**VOICEOVER**:
"Meet ModPulse. An AI-powered contextual moderation app that runs natively inside Reddit. One moderator with ModPulse does the work of fifty."

---

## SCENE 3: Creating a Post — Trigger Fires (12s)
**SCREEN**: Create a new post on r/modpulse_intel_dev. Show the PostCreate trigger activating.
**VOICEOVER**:
"When any user posts or comments, ModPulse automatically kicks in. It tracks the user's activity, stores their karma and account age from Reddit's API, and calculates an eight-factor risk score — all in under two seconds."

---

## SCENE 4: Context Menu — Analyze User (15s)
**SCREEN**: Right-click on a post → Show "ModPulse: Analyze User" menu item → Click it → Toast appears with risk score
**VOICEOVER**:
"Right-click any post or comment. Seven powerful actions at your fingertips. Analyze User gives you an instant risk score: account age, karma, warning history, removal history, strike count, burst activity, removal rate, and subreddit diversity — all factored into one number."

---

## SCENE 5: Context Menu — Full Report (10s)
**SCREEN**: Right-click → "ModPulse: Full Report" → Show complete user dossier
**VOICEOVER**:
"Need the full picture? Full Report shows everything: post count, comment count, every warning, every removal, every strike, and the recommended action — allow, watch, flag, or remove."

---

## SCENE 6: Warn & Strike System (15s)
**SCREEN**: Right-click → "ModPulse: Warn User" → Show warning form → Then show "ModPulse: Issue Strike" → Show strike escalation
**VOICEOVER**:
"Warn users with a single click. Issue strikes with severity levels: minor for first offenses, major for repeat offenders, critical for severe violations. Three strikes and the user is permanently banned. ModPulse handles the escalation automatically."

---

## SCENE 7: Mod Notes (8s)
**SCREEN**: Right-click → "ModPulse: Add Note" → Then "ModPulse: View Notes"
**VOICEOVER**:
"Add notes that your entire mod team can see. Context that follows the user across every interaction. No more 'what did this person do last time?'"

---

## SCENE 8: Background Automation (10s)
**SCREEN**: Show scheduler config, then show daily digest modmail
**VOICEOVER**:
"ModPulse never sleeps. It scans all tracked users every hour, recalculating risk scores. Every morning, your team gets a modmail digest: flagged users, mod actions, and trends. Intelligence delivered automatically."

---

## SCENE 9: The Impact (7s)
**SCREEN**: Show comparison infographic — Manual vs AI
**VOICEOVER**:
"Manual vetting takes ten minutes per user. ModPulse does it in two seconds. That's a three-hundred-X speedup. Your community stays safe. Your team stays sane."

---

## SCENE 10: Call to Action (5s)
**SCREEN**: Show GitHub repo, Devvit app page
**VOICEOVER**:
"ModPulse. AI-powered moderation, built natively for Reddit. Open source. Available now."
